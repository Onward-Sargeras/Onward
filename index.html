About Onward

Are you ready to embark on epic adventures in Azeroth? Look no further! Join Onward, a casual and friendly guild seeking brave souls to bolster our ranks. We are a group of like-minded adventurers dedicated to conquering challenges, raiding with gusto, and having a blast along the way!

Raiding Schedule:

Wednesday, 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM EST

What We Offer:

AOTC Focus: Our primary aim is Ahead of the Curve achievements, taking down bosses and earning those sweet victories together. Ranged DPS Needed: Are you a master of ranged destruction? We’re specifically looking for ranged DPS to round out our raiding team! Social and Mythic Plus Players Welcome: Whether you’re a seasoned raider or new to the scene, a social butterfly, or a mythic plus enthusiast, there’s a place for you in Onward. What We Expect:

Positive Attitude: We value camaraderie, respect, and a positive attitude towards fellow guildmates. Attendance: While we understand real life comes first, consistent attendance for raids is appreciated. Learning and Growing: We encourage a mindset of continuous improvement, both individually and as a team. Why Choose Onward?

Friendly Environment: Join a guild where friendships are forged, laughs are plentiful, and drama is left at the door. Active Discord: Dive into our lively Discord server for discussions, strategies, and memes galore. Guild Events: From transmog contests to achievement runs, we keep the fun going outside of raid nights. Guild Gambling and Lottery: We gamble and have a weekly Lottery to win In game gold! So, if you’re ready to take your place among champions, to face down the toughest foes, and to claim glory in the name of Onward, reach out to us today!


Adventure awaits, heroes! Onward, to victory!

Stacey - Raider

One of the things I love most about Onward is even though we are casual raiding guild we can still come together and achieve AOTC. I enjoy the fact that if I or anyone else in the guild that needs help whether its for gear upgrades or trying to get achievements someone is always willing to volunteer their time to help. I like the fact that we get the chance to vote for what we would like to do together during the "down time" of between seasons. Onward is pretty much drama free, relaxing guild which is what makes it great to be a part of.

Onward is a tight-knit Warcraft guild that has been around for years, dedicated to tackling the toughest challenges the game has to offer. Members of Onward pride themselves on their teamwork and camaraderie, always willing to lend a helping hand to their fellow guildmates. With a strong leadership team and skilled raiders, Onward has consistently cleared content at a swift pace and earned a reputation as one of the top guilds on their server. But beyond their impressive accomplishments in-game, Onward is also known for their welcoming and inclusive community, making it a great place for players of all skill levels to call home. Whether it's strategizing for the next big raid or just hanging out in Discord, members of Onward are always pushing forward together.
